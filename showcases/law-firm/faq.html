Questions clients ask before they call.

Honest answers to the most common ones. If yours isn't here, the consultation is free — ask us directly.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes — the first 30 minutes are free, confidential and carry no obligation. Bring your documents; you'll leave with an honest reading of your position whether or not you engage us.

How are legal fees calculated?

Every fee is agreed in writing before work begins. Defined work — contracts, registrations, reviews — is quoted as a fixed fee. Litigation is staged, so you pay for each phase as it's reached, never for phases that don't happen. There are no surprise invoices.

How long does a court case take in Bahrain?

It depends on the court and the complexity of the matter. Many first-instance civil claims conclude within several months to a year; appeals add time. At the first consultation we give you a realistic range for your specific case — not a best-case number.

Do I need a lawyer for a small dispute?

Not always — and we'll tell you when you don't. A surprising number of small disputes are resolved with a single consultation or one formal letter, at a fraction of the cost of proceedings.

Will my matter stay confidential?

Absolutely. Confidentiality is a professional duty we treat as non-negotiable — your matter is discussed with you and no one else, and that discretion continues after the engagement ends.

Can you handle matters in English?

Yes. We advise, correspond and document in both Arabic and English, and we translate the meaning of every court development into whichever language you're most comfortable in.

What should I bring to the first meeting?

Anything in writing: contracts, messages, notices, court papers, receipts. Incomplete is fine — a timeline of events written in your own words is often the most useful document of all.

I've received a summons. What do I do first?

Call us before responding or attending — the earliest steps in any matter are the hardest to undo. Urgent matters are seen within twenty-four hours.
