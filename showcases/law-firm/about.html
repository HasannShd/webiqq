Managing Partner

Yousif Al Meezan

Yousif founded Meezan & Co in 2013 after six years in one of Manama's leading litigation practices. Since then the firm has acted in more than nine hundred matters — from custody hearings and inheritance settlements to commercial disputes and company formations for founders across the Gulf.

His approach is unfashionably direct. Clients hear an honest reading of their position at the first meeting, including when the wisest advice is not to litigate at all. That candour has made referrals from former clients the firm's largest source of new work.

He appears before all levels of the Bahraini courts, including the Court of Cassation, and advises in both Arabic and English.
