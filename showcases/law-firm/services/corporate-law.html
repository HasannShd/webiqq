How we help

Most commercial disputes are born in a badly drafted document years earlier. We spend our clients' money where it saves the most: on getting the paperwork right the first time.

  • Shareholder, partnership and joint-venture agreements
  • Commercial contracts — drafting, review and negotiation

  • Regulatory compliance and licensing
  • Commercial dispute resolution and arbitration

Our approach

We act as outside counsel to businesses of every size — some call us weekly, some once a year. You get direct access to a partner who knows your file, not a rotating cast of juniors.

What to expect

  1. Consultation

    A free, confidential 30-minute meeting. We hear the full story, review your documents and give you a frank first reading of your position.

  2. Strategy & fee agreement

    You receive a written plan — the route we recommend, realistic timelines, and a clear fee structure agreed before any work begins.

  3. Representation

    We act. You are updated at every development, in plain language, and every significant decision remains yours.
