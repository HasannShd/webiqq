Property is most people's largest decision. Treat it that way.

We review before you sign, register what you own, and act quickly when a lease, sale or off-plan promise goes wrong.

How we help

Bahrain's property market moves quickly, and the documents move quicker. A one-hour review before signing costs a fraction of the dispute it prevents.

  • Sale and purchase agreements — drafting and review
  • Title verification and property registration
  • Residential and commercial lease agreements
  • Landlord–tenant disputes and eviction proceedings
  • Off-plan purchase disputes and developer claims
  • Property inheritance and co-ownership disputes

Our approach

We act for buyers, sellers, landlords and tenants — which means we know how the other side thinks. Every transaction is checked against the registry before a fils changes hands.

What to expect

  1. Consultation

    A free, confidential 30-minute meeting. We hear the full story, review your documents and give you a frank first reading of your position.

  2. Strategy & fee agreement

    You receive a written plan — the route we recommend, realistic timelines, and a clear fee structure agreed before any work begins.

  3. Representation

    We act. You are updated at every development, in plain language, and every significant decision remains yours.
