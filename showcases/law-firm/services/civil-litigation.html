How we help

Whether you are owed money, accused of owing it, or facing a broken agreement, the questions are the same: what can be proven, what is it worth, and what is the fastest lawful route to recovery?

  • Contract disputes and claims for breach
  • Debt recovery and enforcement of judgments
  • Compensation and damages claims
  • Construction and commercial project disputes
  • Insurance claims and coverage disputes
  • Mediation and settlement negotiation

Our approach

Before filing anything, you receive our honest assessment of the claim's strength and its realistic value — including when walking away is the better commercial decision. No client of ours litigates blind.

What to expect

  1. Consultation

    A free, confidential 30-minute meeting. We hear the full story, review your documents and give you a frank first reading of your position.

  2. Strategy & fee agreement

    You receive a written plan — the route we recommend, realistic timelines, and a clear fee structure agreed before any work begins.

  3. Representation

    We act. You are updated at every development, in plain language, and every significant decision remains yours.
