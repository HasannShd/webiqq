# Legitimate listing checklist

## Prepare once

- Exact legal and public trading name and accurate Leading Trading Est. relationship
- Commercial-registration and eligibility details where required
- Approved public address or service area, telephone, email, hours, and markets
- Consistent short and long descriptions and six website service categories
- Logo and real project images with permission
- Verified project URLs and client approval
- Named account owner, recovery email, and two-factor authentication

## Priority order

1. Google Business Profile
2. Bing Places
3. Apple Business Connect
4. Clutch
5. GoodFirms
6. Bahrain Chamber, after eligibility confirmation
7. Sijilat data verification

Request reviews only from genuine clients. Never incentivise a positive review or dictate its wording. Record ownership, verification, live URL, exact entity data, category, and next review date after every submission.
