About Shahbudin

Shahbudin Contracting is one of the Kingdom's most trusted names in the construction industry. We are a certified company for the construction of buildings, project management, infrastructure works, interiors, and electro-mechanical works.

We are committed to delivering exceptional results with the highest standards of quality, safety, and professionalism on every project we undertake.

  • Grade AA certified by the Ministry of Works
  • ISO 9001 Quality Management System certified
  • Committed to health, safety & environmental standards
  • Experienced team of engineers & skilled workforce
